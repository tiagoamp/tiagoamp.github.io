Projects and products developed at Dataprev company

SIRC

Platform to get population data from public notaries all over the country.
These personal data would be used for census, reports, statistics and to support government decisions.
There are about 8 thousand notaries offices which send daily data to SIRC, like birth, marriage and death registrations. These data may be sent by webservices or web-forms provided by SIRC application.
The application is also responsible for process and validate this huge amount of records, building reports and notifications as the output.

Tech & Tools: Java, EJB, JSF, JPA, Spring-Batch, Maven, Oracle Database.

Internet and Intranet modules. URLs:
Portal: http://www.sirc.gov.br/
Application(sign-in needed): https://sirc.dataprev.gov.br/
Desktop application (download): http://www.sirc.gov.br/static/downloads/sirc_cartorio.zip
Webservice wsdl: http://sirc.dataprev.gov.br/SircWs/Movimento?wsdl
